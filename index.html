How to Play

The rules of Rock Paper Scissors are simple:

  • Rock wins against Scissors
  • Scissors win against Paper
  • Paper wins against Rock

If both weapons are identical, it's a draw.

Choose your weapon against the Computer. The first to get to 5 points wins the game.

You can refresh the page or click the "Reset" button in the menu to play again.
